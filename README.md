Fish in a Kettle, _or where tuna and salmon are caught/farmed, and how these parts of the oceans have warmed in the last 25 years_
This visualization uses:
- The UN Fisheries and Agriculture Global Tuna Atlas, which tracks annual fish catches by ton broken down into 1° square of latitude and longitude across the globe. This dataset covers 1950 to 2023.
- NOAA’s GISS Surface Temperature Analysis models, which estimates global surface temperature change. 
- This data is overlaid with the Global Tuna Atlas to show how temperature and tuna catch can geographically correlate

  - Economic Exclusive Zones/Fisheries Claims, which analyzes which countries will be the “winners” of the changing geography of the fisheries
